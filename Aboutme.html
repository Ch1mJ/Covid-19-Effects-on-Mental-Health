About Us

Hello and welcome to our website! We are a non-profit organization that spreads awareness about the effects Covid-19 has brought onto Mental Health. Our mission is to give knowledge or wisdom to others so they can imporve their Mental Health during this Pandemic. We are located in the country of Belize, a third world country that holds breathtaking beauty. We all hope that this website helps individuals to educate themselves about the circumstances our Mental Health can encounter with the Covid-19 virus and how to handle certain overwhelming emotions. Please feel free to make suggestions that we can use as we update our website!


About Foundress

Hello to all, My name is Jada and I am a young woman in the country of Belize. I am currently in school and this is my first website. I created this webiste to spread awareness about Mental Health especially in Belize because I feel like we don't speak about it enough here, this is also the reason I made sure to include how Covid-19 has affected Mental Health in Belize as well. I am a proud Belizean woman who wishes to let everyone in and outside our country know that we are all going through Mental Health issues every day in various ways, so no person is alone! I hope that my research and website can help someone who needs it and I am grateful for any feedback you may leave. Let's be kind to our minds!
